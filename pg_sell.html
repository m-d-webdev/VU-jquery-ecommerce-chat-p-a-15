Welcome in Kratrit

We are here to help you sell your products easily and efficiently. Here’s how the selling process works on our platform:

1. Enter Product Details:

- Fill in all the required information about your product, including the title, description, images, price, and stock. The more detailed your information, the better your chances of making a sale.

2. Listing Your Product:

- Once you’ve entered all the details and they are approved, we will list your product on our platform, making it available to thousands of potential customers.

3. Receiving Orders:

- When your product is sold, we handle the payment process from the buyer.

4. Shipping and Delivery:

- We will coordinate with you to ensure the product is shipped and delivered to the buyer quickly and efficiently. You can rely on our trusted shipping services.

5. Receiving Your Payments:

- After the sale is completed and the product is delivered, we will transfer the payment for the product to your wallet on our platform. You can withdraw the funds from your wallet at any time.

We are committed to providing the best selling experience for you and your customers. If you have any questions or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our seller support team.
